AMBASSADOR BOT

Interactive Buddy Experience

The Ambassador Bot creates a rich interactive experience where Buddy characters actually respond to your submissions. Your content isn't just reviewed by algorithms - it's "seen" by your Buddy, who rates it from 1 to 10 stars and gives you personalized feedback.

Stars & Rewards System

Earn unclaimed Stars based on your content's rating. When you're ready to claim them, there's a chance for a random multiplier - making it as exciting as opening a loot box! Stars can be spent in the bot's shop for invite codes, whitelist spots, and exclusive community rewards.

Role Progression

Level up through community roles based on your XP and contributions. Start as a Newbie, become a Creator, and ultimately reach Visionary status. Each role brings new privileges and recognition in the community.
